Interesting facts about camels

1. They are also known as "Ship of the desert".

2. They drink as much water as possible when they find it, and store them in their stomach.

3. They can even survive "15 days" without water.


Interesting facts about chameleon

1. Their feet work like salad tongs.

2. Almost half of all known species live in madagascar.

3. They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature


Interesting facts about cicada

1. Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.

2. Their lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators.

3. More than 3,000 species exist.


Interesting facts about panda

1. They spend a lot of their day eating.

2. They are found in South-west China.

3. Bamboo is critical to their diet.


Interesting facts about pigeon

1. They are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.

2. They are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

3. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring young ones.


Interesting facts about teddy bears

1. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff

2. The Steiff's Teddy bear in the year 2000 was sold to German for $105,000 !

3. September 9th is Celebrated as National Teddy Bear Day!
